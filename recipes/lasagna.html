Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

It is not too hard to make and will fill. your heart and soul.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onions and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
